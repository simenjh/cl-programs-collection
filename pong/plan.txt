Pong game

MVP
* Two players. Both should be able to control their "racket" through the keyboard.
* The ball should travel at constant speed, and move in a straight line. It should bounce off the walls at opposite angles, eg. 60 and 120 degrees.
* The speed of the ball might increase after a certain play time.
* Display the score.
* Best of eg. 5 wins the game.
* There should be a menu that includes the options:
  - Play now / play again
  - Exit


Architecture
* Scene/state stack
* Separate input from game logic
